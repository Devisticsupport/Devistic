Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website? +

Most standard business websites take between 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. A simple landing page can be done in 5–7 days, while a full multi-page site with custom features typically takes 2–4 weeks. We always agree on a timeline upfront so there are no surprises.

Do you work with businesses outside of your local area? +

Absolutely. While we specialize in helping local businesses, we work with clients worldwide. All our communication and project management is done remotely via video calls, messaging, and project management tools — it's completely seamless.

What do I need to get started? +

Just reach out via our Contact page! We'll schedule a free discovery call to understand your goals, budget, and vision. From there, we'll send over a tailored proposal. You don't need to have any technical knowledge — that's what we're here for.

Will my website work on mobile phones? +

Every website we build is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. With over 60% of web traffic coming from phones, mobile-first design is non-negotiable for us. We test across multiple devices and screen sizes before every launch.

What's included in the Web Care monthly package? +

Our Web Care package includes monthly content updates, plugin/software updates, security monitoring, automated backups, uptime monitoring, and priority email support. It's essentially having us on your team to handle everything technical so you can focus on your business.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes! We offer flexible payment options including split payments for larger projects (typically 50% upfront, 50% on delivery). We understand that budget is a real consideration for local businesses and we're always happy to find an arrangement that works for both parties.
