Our Mission

Built to Solve Real Problems for Real Businesses

We started Devistic because we kept seeing something frustrating: incredible local businesses — hair salons, barbershops, nail studios — losing customers simply because their online presence didn't reflect their real quality.

That's the problem we exist to solve. We bring enterprise-level web development skills and strategy to businesses that deserve them — at prices that make sense.

"Cooked to perfection" isn't just a slogan. It's the standard we hold every project to — every line of code, every pixel, every SEO tag.

Meet the Founder

The Vision Behind Devistic

👤

Henry Benjamin

Founder & Lead Developer

"Every local business has a story worth telling well. My job is to make sure the internet hears it — clearly, beautifully, and loud."

Henry founded Devistic after noticing a consistent gap between the quality of local businesses and their online representation. With a passion for clean code, performance engineering, and user-centered design, he set out to build an agency that genuinely cares about client outcomes — not just deliverables.

When he's not architecting websites, Henry is exploring new web technologies, mentoring junior developers, and finding ways to make the digital world more accessible for independent business owners.
