Our story

From side project
to studio.

Devistic didn't start with a business plan — it started with a laptop, a few late nights, and a client who needed help and couldn't afford a big agency.

2024

Late nights, big idea

What began as a hobby — building practice websites and tinkering with animations — turned into something bigger once we realized how many businesses were stuck with outdated sites that were costing them real customers.

2025

First client, first launch

We rebuilt a website for a friend's business as a favour. The new site loaded faster, ranked better on Google, and started bringing in enquiries within weeks. Word spread fast from there — and so did the client list.

2026

Devistic is born

We formalized everything — a name, a full suite of services, and a proven process we could repeat for any business that needed a digital upgrade. SaaS tools, e-commerce brands, hospitality, real estate, and beyond.
