Pricing FAQs

Questions About Pricing

Are these prices negotiable? +

For small local businesses with tight budgets, we're always open to a conversation. Reach out and tell us about your situation — we'll do our best to find a package that works for both sides. We'd rather work with you than turn you away.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and major credit/debit cards. For projects over $500, we typically split payment: 50% upfront to begin work, and 50% upon final delivery before going live.

Is hosting included in the price? +

Hosting is not included in the one-time package prices, but we can recommend and set up reliable hosting for you (typically $5–$15/month). Our Web Care packages include hosting management but the hosting cost itself is separate. We keep it transparent — no bundling tricks.

Can I upgrade my package later? +

Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter and upgrade as their business grows. We build all sites with scalability in mind, so upgrading is smooth. You only pay the difference between your original package and the new one.

Can I cancel my Web Care subscription anytime? +

Yes — no long-term contracts. Web Care is month-to-month and you can cancel with 7 days' notice before your next billing date. We believe in earning your continued business every month, not locking you in.
